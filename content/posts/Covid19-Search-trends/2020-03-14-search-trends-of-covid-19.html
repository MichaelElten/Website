Importing the data

For this, I will use the R package “gtrendsR” for the first time.

library("dplyr")
library("readr")
library("gtrendsR")
library("googlesheets4")
covid_data %>%
  filter(provincial_case_id==100) %>%
  select(province, date_report)
## # A tibble: 4 x 2

So Ontario had its 100th case on March 14th, BC on March 16th, Alberta on March 18th and Quebec on March 19th.
